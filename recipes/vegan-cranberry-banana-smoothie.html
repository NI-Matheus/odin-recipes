Vegan Cranberry Banana Smoothie

Description

Want a great way to use up leftover cranberry sauce? Try this vegan banana smoothie that uses dairy-free yogurt and almond milk!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup plain vegan yogurt
  • 1/3 cup whole-berry cranberry sauce
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place banana, vegan yogurt, cranberry sauce, almond milk, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving:

  1. 349 calories
  2. 8.1g protein
  3. 68.9g carbohydrates
  4. 6.6g fat
  5. 72.2mg sodium
