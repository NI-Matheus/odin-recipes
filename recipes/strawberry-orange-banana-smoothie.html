Strawberry Orange Banana Smoothie

Description

Orange, banana, and strawberries make up this refreshing smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Blend the orange juice, strawberries, banana and ice cubes in a blender until smooth and frothy.
  2. Enjoy your refreshing smoothie.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving:

  1. 238 calories
  2. 3.6g protein
  3. 58.2g carbohydrates
  4. 1.2g fat
  5. 8.3mg sodium
