Milk Banana Smoothie

Description

Low fat, sugar free, milk banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 and a 1/2 bananas
  • 5(1 gram) packets low calorie granulated sugar substitute

Steps

  1. Blend milk, bananas, and sugar substitute in a blender or food processor until smooth.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving:

  1. 280 calories
  2. 10g protein
  3. 56.8g carbohydrates
  4. 5.4g fat
  5. 101.8mg sodium
  6. 19.5mg cholesterol
